Health FAQs

What are the benefits of eating fruits and vegetables?

Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which are essential for overall health and well-being.

How much water should I drink daily?

The recommended daily water intake varies depending on factors such as activity level and climate. It's generally advised to drink plenty of fluids throughout the day.
